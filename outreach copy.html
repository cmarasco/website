About Me

Hello! I am a second-year Astronomy PhD student at the University of Florida, working with Dr. Jamie Tayar in asteroseismology and stellar ages. Before then, I graduated summa cum laude from Georgia Tech with a Physics B.S. in 2022. Outside of research, I enjoy astronomy outreach, baking, watching movies, and hanging out with my cat!

I am particularly passionate about conducting research in stellar astronomy and high-energy astrophysics. If you'd like to read more about my research, please check out my page about it here.
